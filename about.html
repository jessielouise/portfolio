About Me

Hi, I am Jessie Harris. I am a UI/UX design student and artist who's passionate about creating digital experiences that feel intuitive and inclusive. I am currently earning my B.S. in Psychology at Old Dominion University, with a Minor in The Impact of Technology and a Certification in Human Factors. I genuinely love exploring where design, psychology, and art meet.

As an autistic woman in STEM, I care deeply about accessibility and inclusivity. I really value experiences that are not only aesthetically pleasing, but functional for everyone and also empathetic.

Outside of design, I spend my time drawing (which still counts as design, honestly), coding, playing video games, and exploring the internet. I find a lot of inspiration in how people interact and express themselves online. The same goes for art and games! Both inspire how I think about visual storytelling, emotion, and interactivity. I love how creative spaces, digital or traditional, make people feel something.
