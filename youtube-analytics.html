YouTube Viewer Analytics

Personal viewing analytics feature that allows YouTube users to track their own watching habits, preferences, and viewing statistics

Duration: 4 weeks
Role: UX/UI Designer
Tools: Figma, Data Visualization, User Research
Personal Analytics Data Visualization Behavioral Insights

Project Overview

YouTube users watch countless hours of content but have no way to understand their own viewing patterns and habits. This project creates a personal analytics dashboard that gives viewers insights into their watching behavior, similar to Spotify Wrapped but for YouTube consumption.

The Problem

YouTube viewers have no visibility into their personal viewing statistics, time spent watching, genre preferences, or viewing trends over time, missing opportunities for self-awareness and content discovery.

The Solution

A comprehensive viewer analytics feature that tracks personal YouTube consumption, visualizes viewing habits, and provides insights about preferences, binge-watching patterns, and content discovery trends.

Design Process

1. User Research

Interviewed YouTube viewers about their curiosity regarding their own viewing habits and desired insights.

2. Data Mapping

Identified what viewing data YouTube already collects and how it could be presented meaningfully to users.

3. Information Architecture

Organized personal viewing data into digestible categories: time spent, genres, channels, viewing patterns, and trends.

4. Wireframing & Prototyping

Created dashboard layouts that make personal viewing statistics engaging and easy to understand.

5. Visual Design

Created engaging data visualizations that make personal viewing statistics interesting and shareable.

Key Features

Viewing Time Dashboard

Visual breakdown of total hours watched, daily/weekly patterns, and viewing streaks with engaging progress indicators.

Content Preferences

Analysis of favorite genres, channels, and content types with personalized recommendations based on viewing habits.

Viewing Patterns

Insights into peak viewing times, binge-watching behavior, and how viewing habits change over time.

Discovery Insights

Track how you discover new content, what types of videos lead to rabbit holes, and channel discovery patterns.

Results & Impact

This feature concept gives YouTube viewers valuable self-awareness about their consumption habits while creating opportunities for better content discovery and platform engagement. The design transforms passive viewing data into engaging personal insights.

User Benefits

  • Self-awareness about viewing habits and time management
  • Discovery of viewing patterns they weren't conscious of
  • Improved content recommendations based on detailed preference analysis
  • Shareable viewing statistics similar to Spotify Wrapped
  • Motivation to explore new content categories

Learning Outcomes

  • Learned to design for personal data visualization and behavioral insights
  • Gained experience making complex viewing data accessible and engaging
  • Developed skills in creating shareable, social-ready data presentations
  • Enhanced understanding of user psychology around personal analytics
