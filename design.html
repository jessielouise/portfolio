Smart Crosswalk Light Redesign

Problems: I always get impatient at lights not knowing when it will be my turn to cross. Also, traffic lights don't make any money, they just degrade and cost electricity. With the addition of ads, the lights may be able to pay for themselves. Saving money for the taxpayer.

Redesign for crosswalk signals featuring a digital screen with wait timer, advertising display, and integrated speakers for accessibility. The high contrast display ensures readability in all lighting conditions. This concept addresses user frustration while creating a sustainable revenue model and ensuring inclusive design for visually impaired users.
